Privacy

isthatday.today collects no personal information. There are no accounts, no logins, and nothing you type into the form is stored anywhere — the configurator runs entirely in your browser and only produces a shareable URL.

Analytics

Anonymous page-view counts via Umami. Umami is cookieless and does not track individual visitors.

Advertising

Google AdSense serves the ad on day pages. Google may set cookies to show personalized ads if you have consented elsewhere on the web; otherwise you will see non-personalized ads. See Google's ads policy, and Google's ad settings to manage your preferences.

Third parties

The site loads resources from Cloudflare (hosting and CDN), Umami Cloud (analytics), Google AdSense (advertising), Google Fonts (for the share preview image) and the Twemoji CDN (for emoji in the share preview image).
